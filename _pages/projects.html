Nefelibeta : Academic Resource and Information Platform for Fudan Students
Final Project for Innovative Experiment of Networks 2019 Spring @ Fudan University, by Xintao Wang and Mingzhe Zhu.
I worked with Mingzhe Zhu to design a web platform for shared information about academic resources for Fudan students . I was responsible for front-end development and front-and-back interface, creating a delicate star-style web page with Javascript, Html, Django, Bootstrap , Ajax and Vue.

Geo-fencing: An application of spatial indexing data structure
Final Project for Data Structure 2018 Fall @ Fudan University, by Xintao Wang.
In this project, we are required to apply data structures to a practical scenario. Specifically, we are supposed to employ a spatial indexing data structure to solve the geo-fencing task, in which there are a great amount of insertion, deletion and query operations about the spatial relation between points and polygons.
Personally, I applied R-Tree as a spatial indexing data structure and ray casting to determine the relationship between dots and polygons.
Alice : A Servant Robot with Gesture Recognition
Project for Robotics and Artificial Intelligence 2019 Summer@ Imperial College London, by Xintao Wang( Team Leader), Jianjian Huang, Zilin Liu, Yirui Liu, Junhu Fu, Gaozhi Liu and Kai Lyu.
In this project, we designed a robot which was able to recognize human gestures through Convolution Neural Networks as orders with a 3D camera and act correspondingly to bring convenience to our users. Virtual marketing strategies were also designed .
Natural Language Processing and Stock Price Predictition
Final Project for Natural Language Processing 2019 Fall @ Fudan University, by Xintao Wang.
In this project, I made predictions about stock price with recent reddit news and historical data by CNN and LSTM, and analyzed textual features of days when stock price increased or decreased.
Fantasy and Adventure – A VR Scenery in Unity
Final Project for Introduction to Virtual Reality 2019 Spring @ Fudan University, by Xintao Wang.
In this project, I made a game-style fantastic scenery with models of monsters such as dragon-head-style cannons, element creatures of ice and thunder, octopuses, a devil and a holy dear. Most models were made and painted on my own with blender and substance painter. Students were allowed a chance to ‘see’ and ‘feel’ the VR world they created.
